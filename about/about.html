Finn Meier / Buitenhavenweg 62C, Schiedam / info@finnmeier.com




Finn Meier


is a functional artist based in Rotterdam.

His work revolves around the exploration of the mind, trying to connect through his own associations to the collective.
His creations challenge ideas and relationships toward material and its context. This exploration is an approach to understand what it is to be human and our relationship towards the world that we live in.

These experiments go from atrocity to tenderness, aiming to create intimate and strong reactions in the viewer. Each Project is a new leap into the unknown - pushing borders of possibility, creating experiments in material that stand in between art and design.
